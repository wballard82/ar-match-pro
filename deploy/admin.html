Demo Playbook
20-minute demo that closes · minute by minute
The 20-minute demo
0:00
Discovery (2 min) — before you touch the screen
"Before I show you anything — tell me how your team matches remittances today." Every pain they mention is a feature you'll show.
2:00
Load their data (3 min)
Ask if they have a sample invoice aging export. Import it live. Say: "I'm going to show you this with real invoice data." This is the moment they lean in.
5:00
Auto Match (2 min)
Click Auto Match. When results appear: "This would have taken your team X hours. It just took 8 seconds."
7:00
Show allocation waterfall (3 min)
Click a multi-invoice payment. Show it tying to zero. "This is the one that usually takes your team the longest — we handle it automatically."
10:00
Exceptions + decisions (3 min)
"Instead of chasing these manually, your team makes a decision and it flows into your SAP posting file automatically."
13:00
Reporting (2 min)
"Your CFO sees this every morning — cash matched, what's stuck, and why." DSO and STP rate land with controllers.
15:00
Privacy (1 min)
"Your invoice and payment files are processed locally in the browser during the core workflow \u2014 they are not uploaded to AR Match Pro for matching. No ERP integration or implementation project required; designed for a lightweight security review." Watch the tension leave the room.
16:00
Pricing + close (4 min)
"Most teams start on Pro — that's the multi-invoice allocation you just saw. $4,599/month billed annually ($55,188/yr), and every engagement starts with a 30-day shadow pilot so you see it on your own files first. If your team saves 10 hours a week, you're already ahead week one." Then be quiet. Let them talk.
