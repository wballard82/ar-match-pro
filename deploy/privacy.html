Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you submit the demo request form, we collect: your name, work email, company name, job title, phone number, ERP system, and estimated invoice volume. This information is used solely to follow up with you about AR Match Pro.

2. Invoice and Payment Data

AR Match Pro processes your invoice and payment records locally, within your browser. Your open-invoice book, bank files, and match results are not uploaded to AR Match Pro to be matched, are not stored by us, and are not sold or shared with third parties. AR Match Pro is a hybrid application: a small number of clearly-disclosed supporting services — account sign-in, and an optional AI parser you invoke by choice — communicate over the network, and are described in the Security & Privacy Data Sheet. You retain control of your financial records at all times.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect app functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process contact/demo-request form submissions and Google Fonts for typography. Billing is handled by annual invoice (ACH or wire) after a signed order form — we do not process card payments on this site. No advertising networks or analytics trackers are used on this site.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email info@armatchpro.com.

6. Contact

Privacy questions? Email info@armatchpro.com.
