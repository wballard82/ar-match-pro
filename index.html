The problem

Manual remittance matching slows down cash application.

Payments rarely arrive clean. One payment can cover several invoices, multiple POs, include short pays, or arrive with bank fees and unclear remittance notes.

📋

Workflow-first design

The 8-step guided workflow surfaces what needs action now: upload bank file → upload invoices → Auto Match → Needs Attention → Review → Approve Reconciliation → Posting Export. No digging through tables.

🔗

Advanced matching logic

Match by Invoice #, PO #, Sales Order #, Delivery #, Tracking #, and amount tolerance — in automatic priority order. Upload a Delivery Report to enrich invoices with tracking data for remittances that arrive without invoice numbers.

🚚

SAP delivery bridge — no bank file needed

When a customer's remittance quotes its own shipment IDs or carrier tracking numbers instead of your invoice numbers, ARMP walks the chain: tracking / shipment ref → Delivery Report → sales order → open invoice. Match invoices, delivery report, and remittance advice together — a bank file is optional, and cash stays flagged unconfirmed until you reconcile it.

🤖

Match Confidence & AI suggestions

Every match shows confidence % and Matched By signals (Invoice #, Amount, Customer Account, Tracking #, etc.). Needs Attention exceptions get suggested resolutions — Cash on Account, Bank Fee, Write-off, Chase Customer, and more.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does AR Match Pro replace my ERP?+
No. AR Match Pro supports your existing ERP workflow by helping Accounting and AR teams match payments to invoices, resolve Needs Attention exceptions, and export posting-ready files.
Does AR Match Pro integrate with my ERP?+
No direct ERP integration is required or needed. Teams import invoice reports and bank files exported from their ERP, run Auto Match inside AR Match Pro, and export posting-ready files back. No IT team, no credentials, no scoping project — you can be live the same day.
Is my data protected?+
Yes. AR Match Pro uses secure login, plan-based access, and controlled workflows. Your team works from exported ERP files — AR Match Pro never requires direct ERP access, credentials, or system integration.
What file types are supported?+
CSV and Excel (.xlsx) are fully supported for invoices, bank files, and delivery reports. Invoice imports use the standardized ARMP Invoice Import Template (downloadable in one click from the import screen) — and native SAP FBL5N exports qualify automatically, no reshaping needed. Bank statement formats (BAI2, CAMT.053), PDFs, and even bank or remittance screenshots are also supported.
Do I need to reformat my ERP exports?+
Usually not. The ARMP Invoice Import Template needs just three required columns — Account, Invoice, and Amount — and SAP FBL5N exports map to it automatically, including the Sales Order carried in Reference Key 3. If a file is missing a required column, the import is rejected before anything changes, with a message naming exactly what’s missing and a one-click template download.
Which plan should I choose?+
Choose Starter if you have 1–2 users transitioning away from spreadsheets and need invoice, PO, and amount matching with basic posting exports, persistent delivery index, and pay-per-use AI parsing (50 free parses/month, $0.05 each after). Choose Pro if you need multi-invoice allocation, SAP Delivery Bridge deterministic matching for ERS/VMI consolidated payments, per-line Cash on Account postings, customer learning profiles, historical trends dashboard, unlimited AI Remittance Parser, SAP F-28 / NetSuite exports, CFO dashboards, and match confidence calibration. Choose Team / Custom if you need multi-user collaboration, role-based permissions, advanced approval workflows, and dedicated onboarding.

About AR Match Pro

AR Match Pro is a browser-based remittance matching tool built for Accounting and AR teams. We help companies match customer payments to invoices in minutes — without ERP integration, IT involvement, or lengthy implementation timelines.

Our mission is to eliminate manual spreadsheet matching for AR teams handling PO-based, multi-invoice, and exception-heavy payment workflows. We focus on one workflow and we do it exceptionally well.

What we do

  • Today's Work worklist — surfaces exceptions, payments to allocate, and export-ready records daily
  • Match payments to invoices by Invoice #, PO #, Sales Order, Delivery, and Tracking
  • Handle multi-invoice payments with automatic subset-sum allocation
  • AI suggestions per payment: duplicate detection, short-pay likelihood, confidence scoring
  • Flag exceptions: short pays, unapplied cash, bank fees, duplicates, missing PO
  • Customer open items view (FBL5N-style) with aging buckets and inline apply actions
  • Reconcile tab: write-off and bank fee fields, ties-to-zero status per payment
  • Export posting-ready files for SAP F-28, NetSuite, or any ERP via CSV
  • CFO Summary and Reports dashboard: cash matched, match rate, unapplied risk, short pays
